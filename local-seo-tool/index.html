Key Local SEO Practices: What to Do & What to Avoid?

‚úÖ Best Practices to Implement

  • Include consistent NAP in footer and contact page.
  • Add LocalBusiness schema with geo coords and hours.
  • Incorporate location keywords in title, meta, headings.
  • Embed Google Maps and use tel/mailto links.
  • Optimize image alts with local descriptors.
  • Link internally to location-specific pages.
  • Maintain natural local keyword density in content.

‚ùå Common Mistakes to Avoid?

  • Inconsistent or missing NAP across pages.
  • Absent local schema or invalid markup.
  • Generic titles/metas without geo-terms.
  • No embedded maps or incomplete contact info.
  • Images without local alt text.
  • Poor internal linking to geo-pages.
  • Keyword stuffing with location terms.

Following these guidelines helps search engines verify your local presence while delivering better user experience for nearby searchers.
