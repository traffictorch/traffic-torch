How to Use These Keyword Suggestions - Step-by-Step Checklist

Once you have your 10–20 AI-generated keyword phrases, follow this practical checklist to turn them into real SEO, AEO, GEO, and AIO improvements.

1 Review & Prioritize Suggestions

  • Scan for long-tail phrases with clear buyer or question intent
  • Highlight ones that match your page’s top 3 topics (if URL was provided)
  • Pick 5–8 strongest candidates for immediate use

2 Map to On-Page Elements

  • Title & H1: Use 1 primary long-tail keyword
  • H2/H3 subheadings: Sprinkle 2–4 related variations naturally
  • Introduction & conclusion: Include question-based phrases
  • Image alt text & internal links: Add conversational variants

3 Optimize for AEO & GEO

  • Add FAQ schema with 3–5 question-style suggestions
  • Write direct-answer paragraphs for top 2–3 phrases
  • Use entity-rich language and unique insights in content
  • Include statistics or lists where suggestions fit

4 Track & Iterate

  • Monitor rankings for primary keywords in Google Search Console
  • Check answer engine visibility (Perplexity, ChatGPT Search)
  • Re-run the tool after updates to find new gaps
  • Build content clusters around related suggestion groups

Follow this checklist and turn the keyword suggestions into measurable SEO, AEO, GEO, and AIO wins.
