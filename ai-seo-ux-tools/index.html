SEO Tools

Show Intro

Traffic Torch’s classic SEO tools deliver proven, advanced analysis focused on semantic entity optimization, search intent alignment, on-page signals, direct competitor benchmarking, and structured data mastery. Core pillars that drive sustainable rankings, rich results, and AI-enhanced visibility across modern search systems.

The Local SEO Tool analyzes your Google Business Profile signals, citation consistency (NAP), review velocity and sentiment, local structured data, and proximity/relevance factors. It delivers a complete local health score, highlights missing citations or optimization gaps, and provides prioritized fixes to boost visibility in the local pack, Google Maps, and local summaries.

The Semantic Entity Extractor & Audit Tool instantly detects named entities on your page and scores coverage, salience, relationships, and on-page practices. It calculates an overall semantic readiness score (0–100), identifies topical gaps versus competitors, and delivers AI-generated, prioritized fixes to strengthen entity signals, topical authority, and performance in entity-aware search, answer engines, and generative AI responses.

The SEO Intent Tool performs a comprehensive intent and quality audit, evaluating how well your content matches user search intent while assessing key trust and relevance signals. It highlights mismatches that cause lower rankings, suggests intent-aligned structural improvements, and provides clear guidance to strengthen topical authority.

The Product SEO Tool delivers a full 360° health score for eCommerce/product pages. It analyzes on-page, technical, content/media, and eCommerce signals (schema, price/availability, reviews, variants, social tags). Provides deep-dive modules, priority fixes and recommendations, and projected ranking gains to maximize rich snippets, CTR, and conversions.

The Keywords Tool analyzes on-page keyword placement and usage with surgical precision. It checks primary and secondary keyword density, semantic variations, placement in critical elements (titles, headings, meta), and overall topical relevance — delivering actionable recommendations to optimize without risking over-optimization penalties.

The VS Tool uncovers competitive keyword gap opportunities by comparing your page directly against top-ranking competitors. It identifies high-value keywords they rank for that you don’t, estimates traffic potential, and prioritizes gaps you can realistically capture with targeted improvements.

The Schema Generator instantly detects existing structured data on any URL and generates clean, SEO-optimized JSON-LD markup. Supports all major schema types (FAQPage, HowTo, Article, Product, LocalBusiness, Organization, BreadcrumbList and more). It highlights implementation gaps, provides copy-paste-ready code with educational explanations, and shows exactly which rich results or enhanced snippets you could unlock — a fast way to boost CTR, voice/AI visibility, and overall SERP real estate.

These seven tools combine to give you a complete, education-focused roadmap for traditional, local, eCommerce, semantic entity, and structured-data-powered SEO excellence. Click any of the cards below to enter your URL and instantly reveal opportunities to outrank the competition.

Frequently Asked Questions – Traffic Torch 360° Toolkit

What is Traffic Torch and why use it for modern search optimization? ▼
Traffic Torch is a free, instant, mobile-first 360° SEO, GEO, AEO & AIO toolkit that delivers comprehensive health scores, deep-dive audits, competitive gap analysis, prioritized AI-generated fixes, and clear educational explanations. It helps websites rank higher in traditional search, AI Overviews, voice search, and generative engines by diagnosing technical, content, UX, and entity issues — then teaching you exactly why they matter and how to fix them quickly. Privacy-first, client-side analysis — no data leaves your browser.
What does the 360° health score actually measure? ▼
The 360° score combines signals across technical SEO, on-page optimization, semantic entities, intent match, UX friction (Quit Risk), schema readiness, AI-search compatibility (AEO/AIO), voice search pillars, content authenticity, and competitive gaps vs top-ranking pages. Tools like Home Page Analyzer, Product SEO Tool, and AI Search Optimization Tool feed into this holistic view — helping you prioritize fixes that move the needle on rankings and user trust.
Is Traffic Torch fully mobile-friendly with day/night modes? ▼
Yes — built PWA-ready (installable, fast-loading), every tool and report is fully responsive and optimized for mobile users. Day/night mode uses gray-800 (light) and gray-200 (dark) text for perfect readability in any lighting. Toggle via the 🌙 icon — ideal for on-the-go audits without eye strain.
How does the AI Content Tool help avoid quality filters? ▼
It detects low perplexity, low burstiness, repetitive n-grams, overused AI-favored vocab (e.g., "delve", "testament", "realm"), and uniform sentence patterns that trigger downgrades in Google/AI systems. You get an authenticity score plus concrete humanization fixes — like adding varied rhythm, personal anecdotes, and bursty phrasing — to restore trust signals and maintain strong rankings.
How does competitive gap analysis (VS Tool) improve my SEO/GEO/AEO? ▼
The VS Tool compares your page head-to-head with top results, highlighting missing entities, weaker intent coverage, thin topical depth, and UX signals. It educates you on why those gaps cause lower visibility — then suggests targeted content additions, structure tweaks, and schema enhancements to close them fast and climb rankings across traditional, AI, and voice search.
