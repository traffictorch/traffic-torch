Answerability

What is Answerability?

The degree to which your page provides direct, concise, and quotable answers right at the beginning. AI engines prioritize pages that immediately satisfy user intent with clear summaries they can cite verbatim in search results.

How is Answerability tested?

Scans opening content for bold formatting, definition patterns, FAQ/HowTo schema, question-style headings, step-by-step language, and sufficient opening length.

Why does Answerability matter?

AI-powered search engines heavily favor pages that provide immediate, quotable answers. Direct answers are the #1 factor for being cited in overviews and generative results.

âœ¨

Structured Data

What is Structured Data?

Machine-readable markup (JSON-LD) that explicitly tells search engines what type of content your page contains. This helps trigger rich results, better understanding, and higher likelihood of being selected as a source.

How is Structured Data tested?

Parses all JSON-LD scripts for presence and relevant types (Article/BlogPosting, FAQPage/HowTo, Person).

Why does Structured Data matter?

Proper markup gives explicit signals that improve understanding and trigger rich features. Pages with relevant schema are significantly more likely to be selected as authoritative sources.

EEAT Signals

What are EEAT Signals?

Visible indicators of Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. These include author information, dates, secure connection, and links to reputable sources â€” critical for AI trust evaluation.

How are EEAT Signals tested?

Detects author bylines, date elements, trusted outbound links, and HTTPS usage.

Why do EEAT Signals matter?

Trust is the primary deciding factor for AI citation. Clear authorship, dates, and credible references prove the content comes from a reliable, experienced source.

ğŸ“‹

Scannability

What is Scannability?

How easily both humans and AI can quickly extract key facts from your page. Well-structured content with headings, lists, tables, and short paragraphs is far more likely to be parsed and cited.

How is Scannability tested?

Counts headings, lists, tables, and short paragraphs for optimal density.

Why does Scannability matter?

AI engines prioritize content that can be quickly and accurately extracted. Well-formatted elements like lists and tables are easiest to parse and reuse in answers.

Conversational Tone

What is Conversational Tone?

Writing that feels natural and human by directly addressing the reader, using personal pronouns, asking questions, and acknowledging pain points. This matches how real people search and communicate.

How is Conversational Tone tested?

Counts "you" addresses, personal pronouns, questions, and pain point keywords.

Why does Conversational Tone matter?

Natural human language closely matches real user queries. Conversational writing feels authentic and builds reader connection â€” both valued by modern search systems.

Readability

What is Readability?

How simple and clear your writing is to understand and summarize. High readability (good Flesch score, varied sentences, active voice, simple words) makes content easier for AI to process accurately.

How is Readability tested?

Calculates Flesch score, sentence variation, passive voice usage, and complex word ratio.

Why does Readability matter?

Clear, simple writing is easier for AI to accurately summarize and cite. High readability reduces misinterpretation and improves overall content quality perception.

Unique Insights

What are Unique Insights?

Original, first-hand information that can't be found elsewhere â€” personal testing, case studies, interviews, or timely observations. This prevents de-duplication and boosts perceived value.

How are Unique Insights tested?

Scans for first-hand phrases, dated references, quotes/interviews, and content depth (word count).

Why do Unique Insights matter?

Original first-hand information stands out from generic content. Unique value prevents de-duplication and positions your page as an authoritative primary source.

Anti-AI Safety

What is Anti-AI Safety?

Subtle human writing patterns like natural sentence variation, low repetition, and unpredictable structure. These help avoid being mistakenly flagged as low-quality AI-generated content.

How is Anti-AI Safety tested?

Measures sentence variation (burstiness), word repetition, and predictable sentence starts.

Why does Anti-AI Safety matter?

Human-like variation helps avoid accidental filtering as low-quality generated text. Natural patterns maintain visibility while preserving authentic voice.
