Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Traffic Torch.

What is Traffic Torch? ↓

Traffic Torch is a free, privacy-first suite of instant 360° SEO, GEO, AEO & AIO analysis tools. It delivers health scores, deep educational audits, priority fixes, and predictive forecasts directly in your browser.

Who created Traffic Torch? ↓

Traffic Torch was founded and is maintained by Ylia Callan, a web designer, researcher, and creator passionate about privacy-first tools, SEO correlation research, and modern web experiences. See the Creator section above for full bio and links.

Is Traffic Torch a physical product? ↓

No. Traffic Torch is a digital toolkit only. It is not related to any physical traffic torches, LED batons, or safety equipment. See our “What Traffic Torch Is NOT” section for full disambiguation.

Is Traffic Torch free? ↓

Yes — every tool on traffictorch.net is completely free to use. No sign-up, no paywalls, no data collection. We also offer free browser extensions and plugins for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, WordPress, VS Code, and Framer.

What platforms does Traffic Torch support? ↓

Traffic Torch works on any modern browser (desktop & mobile). Official extensions and plugins are available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, WordPress, VS Code, Framer, and GitHub Actions. All tools are fully responsive and PWA-ready.

How private is Traffic Torch? ↓

100% private. Every analysis runs client-side in your browser using secure proxy fetching only when needed. No data is stored, logged, or shared with us or any third party. This is a core philosophy of the project.
