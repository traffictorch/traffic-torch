6 High-Impact Moves to Boost Voice Search Rankings

1
Target conversational long-tail keywords
Use natural question phrases ("how to", "what is the best", "near me") in headings and content to match how people speak to voice assistants.
2
Write concise, direct answers (40-60 words)
Structure key paragraphs to answer questions immediately and clearly—voice assistants read short, factual responses aloud as top results.
3
Add question-based headings & FAQ schema
Use H2/H3 with natural questions and implement FAQPage/HowTo schema to increase position-zero snippet and AI Overview eligibility.
4
Boost entity coverage & authority signals
Include named entities (brands, locations, people) and cite sources to improve AI recognition and citation in voice results.
5
Maintain positive/neutral sentiment & accuracy
Avoid negative tone and speculative language to reduce hallucination risk and ensure trustworthy voice assistant mentions.
6
Make site fast, mobile-first & schema-ready
Optimize Core Web Vitals, mobile usability, and speakable schema so voice assistants can quickly read and cite your content.
